Hi! It's been a long time since I don't design a LO, but the KDS kit I bought last month say "layout" everytime I looked at it, hahaha, so finally I decided to start a new LO project. 
The "main protagonist" is Pipa, a cat I take care some months ago. I put a little glasses just to make the layout a little bit funny. I also used some spray inks just to give more color to the background. 
As always, you can check the suppliest list at the end of this post.
